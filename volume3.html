Tori's Adventures: Volume 2

Fun Places To Go: With Family and Friends is a book about different and fun places to have fun. Children with disabilities can have as much fun as any child if not more. We went to visit Catalina Island and SeaWorld as a family. We also visited Disneyland and Adventure City with our friends and family. We took along our camera and had a wonderful time everywhere we went.

Through our visits to differents places Tori met all different types of people. She loved everyone the same. Her laughter and smiles meant so much to all around her. Through her eyes anything is possible. Tori rarely fears anything, but one character took her by surprise. Everywhere we go she is the first to run and hug the characters except in one case.
