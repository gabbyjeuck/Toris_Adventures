Tori's Adventures: Volume 2

Early Intervention: Fun with Cristal is a book about Early intervention which is a very important part of a special needs child's early start in life. These teachers help with all their disabilities by turning learning time into fun time so the child learns while they play. Early intervention is a program for children from birth to 3 years old and is geared to help parents to help their child grow and get stronger. Cristal was one of Tori's teachers and stayed with her as a friend beyond 3 years old.

We still have a wonderful relationship with her today. She listened to my concerns regarding Tori's progress and then implemented fun time to compensate where my concerns lied. Cristal is very informed with all special needs, it just happens Tori has Down syndrome and Tori was her first child with Down syndrome. Cristal and Tori became attached to each other. Tori trusted her and so did I. This book will also show you the fun time can be outside. You are not constrained to only inside the house.

Tori always has such a wonderful time with life and playing outside is just an added benefit for her. She loves the outdoors but is confined to indoors throughout the winter months. She has asthma to such an extreme that those winter months really don't allow her to leave the house. As with all she does this too doesn't seem to bother her a bit. She is a very happy little girl.
